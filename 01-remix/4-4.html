Homework,

I love

you.

I think that

you're

great.

It's

wonderful

fun

when you

keep me

up late.

I think

you're

the best

when I'm

totally

stressed,

preparing

and cramming

all night

for a test.

Homework,

I love

you.

What more

can

I say?

I love

to do

hundreds of

problems

each day.

You

boggle

my mind

and you

make me

go blind,

but still

I'm

ecstatic

that you were

assigned.

Homework,

I love

you.

I tell

you,

it's true.

There's nothing

more

fun or

exciting

to do.

You're

never

a chore,

for it's you.

I adore.

I wish

that our

teacher

would

hand you

out more.

Homework,

I love

you.

You

thrill

me

inside.

I'm filled

with

emotions.

I'm fit

to be tied.

I cannot

complain

when you

frazzle

my brain.

Of course,

that's

because

I'm completely

insane.
